about

I'm a PhD Student in the Potioc team at Inria, Bordeaux, working under supervision of Yvonne Jansen and funded by the Ember project. I study predictive visualizations for personal decision making, which are uncertainty visualizations showing predictions of plausible future event outcomes that assist decisions taken by individuals regarding their private life.
I'm into all kinds of maps and big orange circles.
